Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 6, 2025

APRS.Chat is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

  • APRS Messages: We store APRS messages that you send and receive via our service.
  • Account Credentials: Your password is securely hashed and stored. Your APRS passcode is stored for authentication of your account.

How We Use Your Data

  • We do not log into your APRS account or monitor your APRS-IS activity directly.
  • We monitor the APRS-IS network as callsign K8SDR-10 and watch for activity matching your callsign, enabling message delivery and inbox updates.

Your Rights

  • You can export your APRS.Chat data at any time.
  • You can delete your account and all related data whenever you choose.

Data Security

  • Passwords are stored as secure hashes and are never saved in plain text.
  • We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data rights, please contact us at info@aprs.chat.

By using APRS.Chat, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
